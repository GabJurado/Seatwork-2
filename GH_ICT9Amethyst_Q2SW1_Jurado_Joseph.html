Loveable Dog Breeds

Newfoundland These dogs make an excellent companion and are known for their calm and loving nature. If you don't like small dogs and want a loving pet, then these gentle giants may be the one for you.
Bulldog Bulldogs are well-known for their sweet and docile personality. They are very loyal and reliable, and if you're looking for a kind pet then this is a very good option for you!
Great Dane Don't let their size fool you into thinking they're violent, Great Danes are amazing huggers. They are extremely friendly and dependable and are comfortable to have around you. If you're looking for something to cuddle, then these Great Danes are probably for you!
